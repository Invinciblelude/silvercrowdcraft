2.1 — FRAMING: Sill plates & first walls

Prerequisites

Drawing refs known; access ready; safety set; materials staged.

Materials

Lumber, Hangers/straps, Sheathing, Blocking

Tools

Saws/drills/nailers, Levels

Steps

Layout; plates; studs

QC minimums

Plumb/level/flat ≤1/4" in 10', Nailing per schedule, Hangers fully nailed, Fireblocking complete

Safety

Fall protection, Ladder safety

Inspections/Docs

Record inspection/test IDs and dates; pre-cover photos; update as-builts.
