ROOF-002: Underlayment & Flashing

Trade Roofing
Duration 16 hours
Crew Size 2 people

üìã Prerequisites

  • Roof sheathing complete

üß∞ Materials Needed

  • Roofing felt (30lb) or synthetic underlayment
  • Ice and water shield (eaves and valleys)
  • Drip edge flashing (eave and rake)
  • Step flashing for chimneys/walls
  • Roofing nails or cap nails

üîß Tools Required

  • Hammer
  • Utility knife
  • Chalk line
  • Tin snips
  • Harness and lifeline
Fall protection (100% tie-off)
Hard hat
Non-slip boots
Safety glasses
Gloves

üìù Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Install ice and water shield at eaves (6ft minimum, or per code)
  2. Install drip edge at eaves over underlayment
  3. Roll out underlayment horizontally starting at eave, overlap 6 inches
  4. Fasten underlayment with cap nails or staples
  5. Install ice and water shield in all valleys
  6. Install step flashing at walls and chimneys (weave with shingles later)
  7. Install drip edge at rakes over underlayment
  8. Ensure underlayment is wrinkle-free and secure

Ice and water shield covers eaves and valleys completely
Drip edge installed correctly (eave edge under, rake edge over)

No tears or gaps in underlayment
