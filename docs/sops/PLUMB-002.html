PLUMB-002: Water Supply Rough-In

Trade Plumbing
Duration 32 hours
Crew Size 2 people

üìã Prerequisites

  • Framing complete
  • Underground plumbing complete

üß∞ Materials Needed

  • PEX or copper pipe (3/4 inch main, 1/2 inch branches)
  • PEX fittings and manifold (if using PEX)
  • Copper fittings and solder (if copper)
  • Pipe hangers and straps
  • Shut-off valves
  • Water heater (50-gal typical)
  • Hose bibs for exterior

üîß Tools Required

  • PEX crimper or expansion tool
  • Pipe cutter
  • Torch and solder (if copper)
  • Drill
  • Hole saw
Safety glasses
Gloves
Fire extinguisher nearby if soldering

üìù Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Connect main water line from meter to house (3/4 inch or 1 inch per plan)
  2. Install water heater in designated location (on pan with drain)
  3. Run 3/4 inch hot and cold lines from water heater to central manifold
  4. Run 1/2 inch branch lines from manifold to each fixture (sink, toilet, shower, etc.)
  5. Secure pipes to studs with hangers every 6ft
  6. Drill through studs (center) for horizontal runs, nail plates if within 1-1/4 inch of edge
  7. Stub out hot and cold supplies at fixture locations (height per code)
  8. Install shut-off valves at each fixture stub
  9. Install hose bibs at exterior walls
  10. Pressure test system (50 PSI for 24 hours, no leaks)

‚úÖ Quality Control Checks

All pipes are secured properly (no sagging)
Hot is on left, cold is on right at fixtures
Shut-off valves installed at all fixtures
No leaks during pressure test
Pipes protected from freezing (insulation or wall cavity depth)
