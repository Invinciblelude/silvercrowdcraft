üìù Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Call 811 at least 2 days prior to confirm underground utilities marked
  2. Excavate to depth specified on foundation plan (typically 12-18 inches below grade for footing)
  3. Ensure bottom of excavation is level and compacted
  4. Remove any loose soil, roots, or organic material from bottom
  5. Compact subgrade with mechanical compactor (minimum 95% compaction)
  6. Grade site for drainage away from foundation (2% slope minimum)
  7. Stockpile topsoil for later landscaping use
