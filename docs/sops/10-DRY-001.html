üìù Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Hang ceiling drywall first (perpendicular to joists)
  2. Screw every 12" on joists, 16" in field
  3. Hang wall drywall (vertical preferred)
  4. Stagger seams (don't align with corners)
  5. Install corner bead on outside corners
  6. Apply first coat of mud with tape
  7. Let dry 24 hours
  8. Apply second coat (feather edges)
  9. Let dry, apply third coat
  10. Sand smooth when dry
  11. Apply texture if specified
