Phase 3: Foundations
Duration: 3-4 months | Risk: 🟡 MEDIUM

Excavation, footings, slabs, underground plumbing

Geotechnical Requirements:
• Remove 12-18" topsoil and recompact
• Expansive soils (low-medium expansion)
• Geotechnical observation MANDATORY ($20K)
Phase 4: Vertical Construction
Duration: 10-12 months | Risk: 🔴 HIGH

Framing, roofing, MEP, finishes, landscaping

Hurdles:
• Labor shortage (Sacramento market competitive)
• Supply chain delays (windows 8-12 weeks, cabinets 8-16 weeks)
• Inspection backlog (2-3 weeks per inspection)
Cost Risk: $100-200K overruns likely
Phase 5: Marketing & Sales
Duration: 3-6 months | Risk: 🔴 HIGH

Pre-marketing, staging, listings, escrow

Hurdles:
• Market timing risk (prices declining)
• Absorption: 1.5-2 units/month = 3.5-4.5 months minimum
• Buyer financing (7%+ rates limit pool)
Could extend to 12+ months in soft market
